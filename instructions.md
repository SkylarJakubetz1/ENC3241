# Instructions

# How to Wrap a Gift

Introduction: An easy-to-follow guide for wrapping a boxed gift.

# Step 1: Unroll the wrapping paper face down

- Unroll the wrapping paper about the length of the gift.
  
# Step 2: Place the gift in the center of the wrapping paper, face down
- Leave enough space on all sides to be able to cover the top completely.
  
Note: Adding extra inches of space is encouraged.

# Step 3: Cut a straight line along the roll. 

•	Leave the same amount of space between the gift and the roll. Similar to the other sides.

# Step 4: Tightly pull the two long sides of the paper together over the gift

Note: If the gift is a symmetrical box, it does not matter which side you pick to fold over first.

# Step 5: Tape down one side of the paper to the gift, then tape the other, overlapping the bottom piece slightly.

Note: While taping the sides down, keep the paper smooth on the gift

# Step 6: Fold the right and left edges of one open side inward.

•	Find the open sides of the wrapping paper

•	One at a time, fold the left and right sides in and tape them to the gift

# Step 7: Fold the top and bottom pieces together to overlap the right and left edges

•	Push the top side of the paper down and tape it to the gift, then push the bottom side up and tape it.

# Step 8: Repeat on the opposite side of the gift

# Step 9: Crease along the edges of the gift

•	Take your thumb and pointer finger and pinch lightly along the edges of the box to crease the sides
